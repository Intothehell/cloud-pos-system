Retail (Cash/Card):

Refund: Money back to customer, item goes back to inventory

Replacement: No money back, swap damaged item for same item from inventory (stock -1 for replacement, +1 for returned)

Partial return: Customer bought 3 items, returns 1 - refund for that 1 item, restock that 1 item

Wholesale Cash/Card:

Same as retail - cash/card refund or replacement

Wholesale Credit:

Refund: Reduce their credit balance (not cash back), restock item

Replacement: No balance change, swap items in inventory
